    Each student in the school is given a computer to use for the school year. Sadly, one of our younger students got a teacher to help him get into a restricted website and gave a hacker all of the schools information. The hacker is in all of the school websites and laptops. Because of this laptops will be taken away for security purposes. As soon as this hacker is dealt with laptops will be returned.

Sean Parker

Sean Parker is an American entrepreneur and philanthropist, most notable for co-founding the file-sharing computer service Napster, and serving as the first president of the social networking website Facebook. He also co-founded Plaxo, Causes, Airtime.com, and Brigade, an online platform for civic engagement. He is the founder and chairman of the Parker Foundation, which focuses on life sciences, global public health, and civic engagement.
